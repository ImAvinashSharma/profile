Nginx (pronounced "engine X", /ˌɛndʒɪnˈɛks/ EN-jin-EKS), stylized as NGINX, nginx or NginX, is a web server that can also be used as a reverse proxy, load balancer, mail proxy and HTTP cache. The software was created by Igor Sysoev. Nginx is free and open-source software, released under the terms of the 2-clause BSD license. A large fraction of web servers use NGINX, often as a load balancer. A company of the same name was founded in 2011 to provide support and Nginx Plus paid software.In March 2019, the company was acquired by F5 Networks for $670 million.
